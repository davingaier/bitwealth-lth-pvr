Methodology & Assumptions
Portfolio model: Monthly returns are extracted from the LTH PVR historical back-test (BitWealth proprietary simulator). For each monthly interval the pure return is isolated by removing the $200 contribution effect: r = (nav_next − $200) / nav_prev − 1. This return sequence is then applied to a fresh $2,400 portfolio with $200/month additions. For sub-periods (1yr, 3yr, 7yr) the most-recent N months of the 5yr or 10yr dataset are used, rebased to a fresh start.
Management fee: 1.0% p.a., charged monthly = NAV × (rate / 12) at end of month.
Exchange fee sharing: 50% of VALR fees. USDT/ZAR blended 0.265% (maker 0.18% / taker 0.35%); BTC/USDT & USDPC/USDT blended 0.090% (maker 0.08% / taker 0.10%). Fee applies to: ZAR→USDT conversion of each contribution, BTC/USDT DCA buy, USDPC/USDT idle-USDT sweep.
Current fee comparison: 0.75% platform fee on each contribution (upfront); 10% performance fee on monthly profit above high-water mark (HWM resets to new NAV peak).
Disclaimer: Back-test data is from June 2016 – June 2026. Past performance does not guarantee future results. This model is for commercial planning purposes only.
