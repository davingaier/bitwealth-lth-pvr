BitWealth makes sophisticated Bitcoin investment strategies accessible to everyone. We combine on-chain intelligence, automated dollar-cost averaging, and a dynamic sizing engine into a single rules-based, fully-auditable strategy — helping clients build long-term wealth through Bitcoin without the emotion, guesswork, or constant monitoring.

Long (≈ 90 words)

BitWealth was founded to make sophisticated Bitcoin investment strategies accessible to everyone. Our flagship LTH PVR strategy reads Long-Term Holder behaviour directly from the Bitcoin blockchain to judge when BTC is statistically cheap or expensive, then combines patient dollar-cost averaging with a dynamic grid engine that scales each order to conviction. The result is a disciplined, transparent, and fully-automated approach that accumulates in weakness and trims in strength. Every decision is rules-based and auditable, and clients track performance in real time through a dedicated portal.

05 — Product

LTH PVR Bitcoin DCA Available now

Smart Bitcoin accumulation using on-chain intelligence. A rules-based strategy that capitalises on BTC market cycles by monitoring Long-Term Holder behaviour and the profit-to-volatility ratio. Automated, transparent, and fully auditable — BTC/USDT.

1On-Chain Signal
LTH PVR metric

The Long-Term Holder Profit-to-Volatility Ratio reads the “smart money” cohort directly from blockchain data — a manipulation-resistant, cycle-aware gauge of when BTC is cheap or expensive, measured in standard deviations from its mean.

2Disciplined Accumulation
Dollar-cost averaging

Automated contributions on a regular cadence remove entry-point risk and emotion. Cost basis converges to the mean over the contribution window rather than any single day's price.

3Adaptive Execution
Dynamic grid logic

Sigma bands around the daily on-chain mean form a self-recalibrating grid. Orders scale to conviction — aggressive buys when oversold, trims when overheated — and never go stale.

4Idle-Cash Yield
Optional · USDPC

Between positions, idle USD can be auto-swept into USDPC targeting ~8–10% APR, then converted back the instant a buy signal fires. Optional, per portfolio.

Why it matters

LTH PVR is engineered for the best risk-adjusted outcome — accumulating patiently when on-chain data says Bitcoin is cheap and trimming when it says Bitcoin is expensive. The goal is a strategy investors can stay invested in through full market cycles, month after month, drawdown after drawdown.
