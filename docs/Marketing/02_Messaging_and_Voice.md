## 5. Value propositions

- **Intelligent, not manual.** Decisions are driven by on-chain data, not gut feel.
- **Disciplined by design.** Automated DCA removes emotion and entry-point risk.
- **Risk-adjusted.** Engineered for a result investors can stay invested in through
  full market cycles — not just the biggest headline number.
- **Transparent.** Rules-based and fully auditable, with a real-time client portal and
  detailed monthly statements.
- **Aligned pricing.** Performance fee only on new profits (high-water-mark protected);
  a modest one-time platform fee; no hidden or management fees.

---

## 6. Proof points & how it works (plain language)

1. **On-chain signal (LTH PVR).** Reads the “smart money” long-term holder cohort
   directly from the blockchain to gauge when Bitcoin is statistically cheap or expensive.
2. **Dollar-cost averaging.** Contributes on a regular cadence, spreading entries through
   time to remove the risk of mistiming a lump sum.
3. **Dynamic grid.** Sigma bands around the daily on-chain mean re-centre every day, so
   orders scale to conviction and never go stale — buying more when cheap, trimming when
   expensive.
4. **Optional idle-cash yield.** Between positions, idle USD can auto-earn ~8–10% APR via
   USDPC and convert back the instant a buy signal fires.
