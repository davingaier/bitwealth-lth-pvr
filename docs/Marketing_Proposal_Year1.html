PRIMARY PERSONA  ·  R1M – R5M INVESTABLE ASSETS
  • Age 35–55 — entrepreneur, senior professional, business owner
  • Already has unit trust / offshore exposure; frustrated by rand depreciation
  • Bitcoin-aware but hasn't acted — lacks structure, time, and expertise to self-manage
  • Values automation, data, and transparency over speculative tips

Why now? South African HNW investors face a structural problem: rand weakness, low real returns on local assets, and limited access to credible, structured crypto exposure. The LTH PVR strategy fills this gap — offering fully-automated, on-chain-signal-driven Bitcoin accumulation via a regulated South African exchange (VALR), with auditable sub-account segregation and no self-custody risk.

The target segment — R1M to R5M investable assets — represents approximately 450,000 South African individuals (ASISA/FNB wealth reports). Even capturing 0.01% of this segment exceeds the Year 1 target.

The product is accessible (R5k/month entry), differentiated (on-chain signal, not price speculation), and the 3–10 year back-test results provide compelling, auditable evidence of out-performance versus both standard DCA and buy-and-hold.
