The Strategy

LTH PVR (Long-Term Holder Profit-to-Volatility Ratio) is a proprietary Bitcoin DCA strategy that dynamically sizes buy and sell allocations based on where the current BTC price sits relative to on-chain derived statistical bands.

The bands are computed daily from Long-Term Holder (LTH) realised price data — a measure of the average acquisition cost held by Bitcoin investors who have not moved their coins in over 155 days. This cohort accounts for over 70% of circulating supply and has historically been the most accurate leading indicator of macro cycle turning points.

The ratio of spot price to LTH realised price, expressed in standard deviations (σ), determines the allocation tier for the day's contribution — buying aggressively when price is statistically cheap relative to long-term conviction, and reducing exposure when price is statistically extended.

Why this signal is distinctive:

  • Derived from blockchain data — not price action, sentiment, or technical indicators
  • Based on aggregate cost basis of real BTC holders, not derivatives
  • Sigma thresholds are calculated from a proprietary smoothed LTH dataset and cannot be replicated from public price feeds alone
  • Resistant to market manipulation — on-chain accumulation cannot be faked
  • Signal generates one decision per day, reducing emotional trading and churn

The strategy executes daily via automated trading pipeline, with each participant's portfolio tracked independently at the sub-account level.

Strategy Variants
Progressive Strategy
MAXIMUM GROWTH ORIENTATION
  • Full 11-tier sigma allocation system active
  • Higher buy percentages in deeply undervalued zones
  • Bear Market Pause preserves capital above +2.0σ — re-enters below −1.0σ
  • Momentum filter suppresses sells in weak uptrends
  • Retrace exception allows counter-trend buys when price corrects from elevated zones mid-cycle
  • Maximises BTC accumulation during bear cycles; captures profit in bull runs
  • Best suited for: Long horizon investors comfortable with higher short-term volatility in exchange for superior growth
Conservative Strategy
RISK-MANAGED ORIENTATION
  • Same full 11-tier sigma allocation system active
  • Reduced allocation sizes across all tiers for smoother NAV path
  • Earlier, smaller sell triggers to capture gains more frequently
  • Same Bear Market Pause and momentum logic for downside protection
  • Earlier accumulation relative to Progressive variant — re-enters below mean
  • More consistent intra-cycle performance — smaller gap between bull and bear years
  • Best suited for: Capital preservation focus, shorter investment horizons, or portfolio diversification role
